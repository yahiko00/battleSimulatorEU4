Notes for this release:
* Only the land battle mechanic is implemented. No siege or naval battle.
* Unit deployment is not implemented. Units are deployed in a one linear single row.
* Unit selection is not implemented. A unit only targets the face-to-face opponent one.
* Attacker and Defender's armies must be exactly of the same size.
* Any bug or improvement suggestion can be sent by email to yahiko (dot) ninja (at) gmail (dot) com.
